EGUSI-SOUP

Description

Egusi soup is one of the most wildly eaten soups in Nigeria. It is a delicious food made with the best ingredients

This soup is a master piece that goes with all kinds of swallow and loved by all.

INGREDIENTS

  • grounded egusi
  • red oil,meat,dry fish and stock fish
  • species like salt,pepper,seasoning cubes,crayfish
  • a vegetable named ugwu

STEPS

  1. watch your meat, dry fish, and stock fish and set aside
  2. watch the vegetable, slice and set aside
  3. put your pot on fire, add the meat,and stock fish with adequate quantity of species excluding the crayfish
  4. allow to steam for some time, add appropriate water for the soup,add the red oil
  5. add the dry fish, crayfish, stir and add in the grounded egusi, allow to cook for some time
  6. taste for need of any specie, add the sliced vegetable and stir well
  7. serve hot with any swallow of your choice and enjoy.
