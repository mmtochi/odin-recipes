RICE

Description

Rice is a carbohydrate, it is a delicious food and it is well acceptable in all countries and it is and eaten world wide.

Rice is an engery giving food. it is among the six primary classes of food we have falling under the family of carbohydrate as mentioned earlier.

INGREDIENTS
  • tomatoes
  • chicken
  • species
  • vegetable oil
  • unboiled rice

STEPS

  1. per boil your rice, wash and keep aside
  2. season the chicken and cook and fry when finally well cooked
  3. cut your tomatoes and fry with vegetable oil till it's dry
  4. add desired quantity of water to the fried tomatoes, add adequate species and taste
  5. add the washed perboiled rice, stir with a woundend spoon to mix well
  6. serve hot with the chicken, and enjoy.
